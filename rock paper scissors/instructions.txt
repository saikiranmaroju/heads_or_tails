Launch the Python Rock, Paper, Scissors game from the command-line interface.
The game will display a prompt to enter your choice Type 0 for Rock, 1 for Paper or 2 for Scissors.
Type your selection and press Enter to make your move.
The computer AI will then make its choice.
The program will announce the result of the round (win, lose, or tie) and update the score accordingly.
Continue playing additional rounds to see if you can outsmart the computer and achieve a winning streak.
Whether you want to reminisce about the classic hand game of your childhood or challenge yourself to strategic decision-making, the Python Rock, Paper, Scissors project guarantees hours of amusement. Sharpen your choices and get ready to prove your dominance in this timeless battle of rock, paper, and scissors!
